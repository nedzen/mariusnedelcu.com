Digital iridescence

  • Print
  • digital

This self-initiated project explores two ideas. The mesmerizing appeal of highly saturated images and that of unbalanced asymmetrical compositions. It is essentially a quest to find those visual cues which attire attention and likes. These artworks were posted on ello and the community reacted to each of it differently. The aesthetic is optimized for visual satisfaction and in some works to intrigue and unset.
